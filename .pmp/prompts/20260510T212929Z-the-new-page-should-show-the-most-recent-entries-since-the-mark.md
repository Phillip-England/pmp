# The "New" Page should show the most recent entries since the mark

Okay when we are submitting new prompts, it is sometimes useful to see the most recent prompts you have entered. So, on the "New" page, imagine we have no new prompts since the last mark, meaning the newest prompt is marked, meaning we just compiled, well then nothing will be seen on the new page other than our form to submit a new prompt. But lets imagine we have 4 prompts that have been created and then our 5th prompt is marked, well those first 4 prompts shall be considered new prompts, and we shall see those prompts in a flex-wrap container on the "New" Page. Do not express to much information, maybe just display the title of the prompts to jog the user and remind them of recent entires.
